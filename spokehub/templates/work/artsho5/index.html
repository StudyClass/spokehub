ARTSHO5

Event Design

November 2014

Gaspar Bonta / Tim Boyce / Kwame Charles / Winston Chmielinski / Carleen Coulter / Dolly Demoratti / Catherine Greig / Tom Horak / Valencia James / Seyhan Musaoglu / Anders Pearson / James Pendergrast / Valerie Schmidt / Onika Simon / An Paenhuysen / Saleem Reshamwala / Richard Watkins

The 5th annual ARTSHO exhibition on the subject of experimentation, a process both artists and scientists share. The team included 8 international artists and an architect, who worked together to convert a derelict space into a makeshift art lab, in under 3 days. The aim of the event itself was to produce 8 interactive artworks with the public in real time. Social media posts with the hashtag #ARTSHO5 were streamed into the lab 24/7 and a 9th project involved transatlantic collaboration between Istanbul, Turkey and Durham, USA.
