Sorry For Laughing

Exhibition

August 2014

Raphael Abrams / Ditte Lyngkaer-Pedersen / Patrick Krawczykowski / Jana Nowack / Valerie Schmidt / Onika Simon

The first installment of a curatorial conversation between contemporary art works and design objects. In this exchange art looks at the shape of society and irritates its boundaries, whereas design takes the functionality of objects in service to society and stretches their limitations. This exhibition featured a photographic series based on an original portrait of friends laughing in Japan in 1956 - juxtaposed with a "twitchie" robot designed to trigger laughter instantaneously.
