Maddie Peterson

Identity

October 2014

Tamara Connolly / Onika Simon

Concept development and creation of a feminist identity strategy for 17 year old surfer and philanthropist Maddie Peterson. Crafting a visual language emphasizing athleticism and social media messaging hierarchy around holistic interests, broke with conventions of the surfing category and broader portrayal of American female athletes overall.
