Fight Club Sandwich

Performance

June 2015

Kwame Charles / Patrick Krawczykowski / Vivian Kvitka / Jana Nowack / Onika Simon

A collaborative performance, choreographed art film, social sculpture composed of hazmat suits and series of photographic prints - Fight Club Sandwich was developed and produced by members of the KN Collective in Berlin, as part of the inaugural Berlin Food Art Week.
