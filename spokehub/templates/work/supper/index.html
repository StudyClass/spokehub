Supper Salon 1

Event

February 2015

Kwame Charles / Winston Chmielinski / Carleen Coulter / Vivian Kvitka / Onika Simon

Symposium and dinner on the subject of Nature & Technology. Subject, menu and attire were designed to reflect the installed exhibition Show Me How at the KN Gallery in Berlin. Guests were curated to include artists, academics and tech entrepreneurs - and their thoughts and ideas were captured and shared on a physical web installation above the dining tables.
